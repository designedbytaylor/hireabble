<analysis>**original_problem_statement:**
The user wants to create a mobile-first, Tinder-style application for job searching, named Hireabble.

**PRODUCT REQUIREMENTS:**
- **Dual User Roles:** The platform must support both Job Seekers and Recruiters.
- **Core Swipe Mechanics:** Job seekers swipe right to apply for a job and left to pass. A match occurs when a recruiter also expresses interest in a seeker who has applied.
- **Recruiter Functionality:** Recruiters can post, view, and edit their job listings. They can also view candidates who have applied to their jobs.
- **Frictionless Onboarding:**
    - User registration should be simple, requiring only basic information (email, password, role).
    - After registration, job seekers are guided through a step-by-step process to build a simple resume by providing details like work experience, education, headline, and a professional photo.
- **Profile & Resume:**
    - User profiles must display the uploaded photo.
    - A feature to download a user's resume as a PDF.
    - A profile completeness indicator for seekers.
- **Job Cards:** The swipe-able job cards must display key information like job title, experience level, etc.
- **Real-time Chat:** Once a match is made, the recruiter and job seeker should be able to chat in real-time within the app.
- **Notifications:** Send email notifications to users when a new match occurs.
- **Forgot Password Feature:** Implement a mechanism for users to reset their forgotten passwords.
